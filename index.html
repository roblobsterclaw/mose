Owner Earnings = Net Income + D&A − Maintenance CapEx (Buffett 1986 method)
Base IV: historical revenue CAGR, 10% discount, 2-stage (Yrs 1-5 full / Yrs 6-10 half rate)
Bear = 60% growth, 11% discount | Bull = 130% growth, 9% discount
Maint CapEx%: High-growth >15% = 55% maint | Moderate 5-15% = 70% | Mature <5% = 85%
MOS = (Intrinsic Value − Price) / IV — positive = undervalued, >25% = BUY zone
